Name:
tsodyks_shortterm_bursts - sli script for testing Tsodyks short term plastic synapses.
Synopsis: 
(tsodyks_shortterm_bursts) run

  'Synchrony Generation in Recurrent Networks with Frequency-Dependent Synapses'  
  Tsodyks, Uziel, Markram  
  JNeuroSci, Vol. 20 RC50 p. 1--5, (2000)  
   
  author:  Moritz Helias  
  date:    March 2006  
   
  see also: brunel.sli
